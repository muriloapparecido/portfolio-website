Overview

GymPal is a mobile app designed to connect University of Michigan students who share similar fitness interests. The app allows users to match with workout partners based on their gym preferences, workout routines, frequency, and experience levels. With GymPal, the goal is to make going to the gym more enjoyable and collaborative by fostering connections among like-minded individuals.
